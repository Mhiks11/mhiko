Book Summaries

The Amazing Spidermann

This is a novelization or comic book adaptation of the origin of Spider-Man,
a teenage boy named Peter Parker who gains spider-like powers after being bitten by a radioactive spider.
Balancing his life as a high school student and superhero, Peter learns the famous lesson: "With great power comes great responsibility."

Harry Potter and the Sorcerer’s Stone

The story follows Harry Potter,
an orphaned boy who discovers on his 11th birthday that he is a wizard.
He attends Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry,
makes close friends (like Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger),
and learns about his magical heritage.
Along the way, he uncovers the mystery of the Sorcerer’s Stone and his connection to the dark wizard Lord Voldemort.

Iron Man

Tony Stark, a billionaire genius and weapons manufacturer,
is gravely injured by a bomb made by his own company.
Captured by terrorists, he creates a high-tech suit of armor to escape and later upgrades it to become the superhero Iron Man.
Using his intellect and technology, Tony fights against threats like corporate corruption, terrorism, and powerful villains—all while struggling with his own personal flaws and legacy.
